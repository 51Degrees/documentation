ShuttingDown 

Last strategy's decision was 'deny'.

Wait for active requests to die off.

WaitingForGreenLight 

Last strategy's decision was 'deny'.

Wait for it to return 'allow'.

Last strategy's decision was 'allow' (after recovery).

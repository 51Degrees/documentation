StarSignBoundary Struct Reference

Detailed Description

One row of the star-sign table.

A sign runs from start (a month and day, inclusive of the day after) up to end. The comparison is on the month-and-day only, the year is irrelevant, so a date is just reduced to its (month, day) before it is tested against the boundaries.
